Virabhadrasana or Warrior pose
  1. Stand straight with your legs hip-distance apart and arms on your sides. Now, spread your legs wide-apart
  2. Turn your left feet out to face the left side of the mat. The other feet will turn inward to make a 45 Degree angle.
  3. Twist your body to left completely. Go forward on your left knee making a 90 Degree angle; make sure that your knee doesn't cross over the toe.
  4. Let the other leg stretch properly- Now go into a Namaste position, and taking our arms up above your head, look up, arch your back a bit and stretch.- Hold for 10 seconds and repeat on the other side.
