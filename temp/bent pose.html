Setu Bandhasana or Bend pose
  1. Lie down flat on your back on a hard surface, preferably on a mat. Bend your knees and make sure to place your legs hip-width apart.
  2. Put your hands beside you with the palm facing downwards. Putting the pressure on the floor through your hands, lift your back.
  3. Lift such that your chin is touching your chest, without any movement or effort. Inhale and lift your torso is little up.
  4. Make sure your knees and ankles are aligned in a straight line. Your thighs should be parallel to the floor
  5. Hold on to your breath for a few seconds and release. Repeat the process a few times.
