Uttanasana or Forward bent pose
  1. Stand straight with your legs hip-distance apart and arms on your sides. Now, spread your legs wide-apart
  2. Relax the body in Mountain Pose taking a few deep breaths. Exhale and move your torso forward from the hips, not the waist.
  3. Continue to bend till your hands touch your feet.
  4. Stretch your hips and bend further (without straining the body). Place the palm of your hands by the side of your feet.
